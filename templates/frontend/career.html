How to apply

  1. Prepare an up-to-date CV and short cover letter describing why you'd be a good fit.
  2. Email your application to careers@citygateglobal.com (subject: Role Title).
  3. Include contact phone number and your location in the body.

You can also reach HR by phone: 0700 003 67473. For general enquiries visit the Contact page.

Send us your CV today — we review all applications and contact shortlisted candidates for interviews.
