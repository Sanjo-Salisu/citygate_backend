Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 February 2024

Empire Trust Mfb App, powered by Empire Trust Microfinance Bank Ltd values the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting their personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through Empire Trust Mfb App (“Empire Trust Mfb“, “our”, “we”, “us”), including but not limited to information provided by you, information automatically collected by us, and information obtained from third-party sources.

Please note that by clicking the “Proceed” button you are consenting to Empire Trust Mfb obtaining information provided by you and from relevant third parties as may be necessary. The collection and processing of your personal data is in accordance with the National Information Technology Development Agency Act 2007 (the “Act”), Nigeria Data Protection Regulation 2019 (the “Regulations”) and the provisions and prescriptions of Section 5; Part 1 and Part 2 of National Information Systems and Network Security Standards and Guidelines.

If you do not agree to this Policy or any part thereof, please do not use or access Empire Trust Mfb App or any part thereof.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: This includes information such as your name, email address, and other contact information provided by you when you create an account on Empire Trust Mfb App or make a transaction.

Location: Empire Trust Mfb may collect information about your device location, including the device’s GPS coordinates or IP address, to provide relevant services. This also helps us find and verify addresses, which assist in speeding up the “know your customer” (KYC) process.

House Address: Empire Trust Mfb may collect your house address to provide location-based services or to verify identity which serves as part of our “know your customer” (KYC) process.

Mobile Device Information: Information we collect from your device includes IMEI number, unique ID, that is, operating system information, SDK version and mobile network information (including operator name, SIM card serial number and SIM card slot), your personal data information, List of installed applications, Wi-Fi list, currently connected WIFI name, available storage space of the device, total storage space of the device. We collect information about your device to provide automatic updates and additional security. In addition, this information provides us with valuable feedback about your identity as the device holder and your device behavior, so that we can improve our services and provide you with an enhanced customized user experience.

Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with Empire Trust Mfb App, including the features you use, how often you use Empire Trust Mfb App, and the duration of your sessions.

Financial Information: Used to process payments and record transactions.

BVN: Used to verify your identity for financial transactions.

Image Information: Empire Trust Mfb may collect images provided by you, such as profile pictures or images uploaded through Empire Trust Mfb App. The image information is used to personalize the user experience by displaying your profile picture in Empire Trust Mfb App and to enhance Empire Trust Mfb App features that involve image recognition.

Mobile Number: Empire Trust Mfb collects your mobile number provided by you during registration. This information is used to send you transaction notifications

Next of Kin Details: Empire Trust Mfb may collect information about your next of kin in order to provide emergency contact information in the event we are unable to contact you.

Work Background & Income Information: Empire Trust Mfb may collect information about your educational background and professional experience to better match you with potential employers or projects.

WhatsApp ID: Empire Trust Mfb may collect your WhatsApp ID to enable secure communication and to provide you with customer support.

How We Use Information

  • Enable Transactions: Processing payments and generating receipts.
  • Personalization: Providing relevant, customized services.
  • Improve Empire Trust Mfb App: Enhancing features and user experience.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We maintain strict confidentiality of your data. Information is only shared under the following conditions:

  • Service Providers: Only where necessary and under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.

Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for legal, regulatory, or service-related purposes. Backup copies may remain in secure storage for compliance purposes.

User Controls

You may access, correct, update, restrict, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at: support@empiretrustmfb.com.

Data Security

We use security measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. In case of a breach, affected users will be promptly notified.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on encrypted, secure servers with controlled access.

Cookies

We may use cookies to store preferences and analyze usage. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

Remedies

Any disputes regarding this policy fall under the jurisdiction of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Claims must be made within one month of the occurrence.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated terms.
