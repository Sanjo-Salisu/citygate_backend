Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10 December 2025

Citygate Global Ltd, is committed to safeguarding your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose your information when you access or use the Citygate Global mobile application and related digital services.

By clicking “Proceed” or using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy and agree to comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and all applicable laws.

If you do not agree with these terms, kindly discontinue use of the Citygate Global App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, date of birth, and account details.

Location Information: GPS data or IP information used for identity verification.

Residential Address: Required for profile verification and service delivery.

Mobile Device Data: IMEI, device ID, SIM details, OS version, installed apps list, storage capacity, and connectivity data.

Usage Information: Pages visited, buttons clicked, time spent, and in-app behavior.

Image Data: Profile picture, identity verification photos, or documents.

Next of Kin Details: For emergency contact and compliance requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • For Account Registration: Creating and maintaining your profile.
  • To Process Transactions: Sending, receiving, and confirming payments.
  • To Verify Your Identity: Ensuring compliance with KYC and AML requirements.
  • To Personalize User Experience: Showing relevant content and features.
  • To Improve App Performance: Monitoring errors, crashes, and performance metrics.
  • For Customer Support: Responding to inquiries and resolving issues.
  • For Security Purposes: Detecting fraud, unauthorized access, and suspicious behavior.
  • For Regulatory Compliance: Meeting legal and audit requirements.

3. Data Protection Principles

We handle your data according to global data protection standards. Your information will be:

  • Processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
  • Collected for valid, specific, and lawful purposes.
  • Limited to what is necessary for service delivery.
  • Accurate and kept up-to-date when required.
  • Stored only for as long as necessary.
  • Protected against unauthorized or unlawful access.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your information. Data may be shared only under these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies supporting app functionality, under confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory Bodies: When required by law or financial regulations.
  • Security Agencies: For fraud detection, risk assessment, or legal investigations.
  • Business Continuity: If required for backup, cloud hosting, or system recovery operations.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The Citygate Global App may use cookies, device identifiers, and tracking tools to improve performance and personalize your experience. You can manage these settings within your device preferences.

6. Data Retention

Your information is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy. This includes:

  • Regulatory retention periods for financial records.
  • Fraud prevention and audit requirements.
  • Active service delivery and customer support needs.

7. Security Measures

We employ advanced security technologies to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information.
  • Multi-factor authentication.
  • Regular security monitoring and audits.
  • Secure cloud storage and backup systems.
  • Strict internal access controls.

8. Your Privacy Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or updates to inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Request deletion of your information (where legally allowed).
  • Request transfer of your data to another provider.
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing.

9. Third-Party Services

Some features in the Citygate Global App may link to external services or APIs. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed in data centers outside Nigeria. In such cases, we ensure compliance with global data protection standards.

11. Children’s Privacy

The Citygate Global App is not intended for individuals under 18 years. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Citygate Global may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified through in-app alerts or updates. Continued use of the app confirms your acceptance of any changes.
